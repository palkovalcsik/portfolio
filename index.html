The Meaning

The second name of the person"Kovalcsik", the Hungarian version of the Slovak "Kováčik", refers to a Smithson or Smisson. It is created from child of the smith in other words son of the smith.

"Kovalcsik" means the son of the man who works in metal. It is related to the word "smitan", the old English form of smite, which also means strike (as in early 17th century Biblical English: the verb "to smite" = to hit).

The First name of the person"Pal" means: friend, form a friendship, companion, main man, amigo, bro.
